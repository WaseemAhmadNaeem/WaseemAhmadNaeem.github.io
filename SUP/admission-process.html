To participate in Skilled-Up Pakistan Skills Development Initiative, students have to adopt the following process.
  • Go to Apply Now page and submit the registration form
  • Your application will be examined and you will receive confirmation email
  • Follow the instruction received in email to pay fee
  • Respond email with Required documents and color scan of fee chalan form
  • Your documents will be reviewed, and you will receive admission details accordingly
  • Application will now be enrolled in Skilled-Up Pakistan Skills enhancement program
